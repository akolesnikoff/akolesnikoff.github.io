Pushing the frontier in image and multimodal models at Google Brain (now DeepMind) since 2018 in the beautiful city of Zurich. Training SOTA vision models ([ImageNet SOTA in 2019](https://arxiv.org/abs/1912.11370), [ImageNet SOTA 2020](https://arxiv.org/abs/2010.11929), [ImageNet SOTA 2021](https://arxiv.org/abs/2106.04560)) and SOTA open weight models: [SigLIP](https://arxiv.org/abs/2303.15343) and [PaliGemma](https://github.com/google-research/big_vision/blob/main/big_vision/configs/proj/paligemma/README.md).

Along the way, inventing new architectures: [BiT](https://arxiv.org/abs/1912.11370), [ViT](https://arxiv.org/abs/2010.11929), [MLP-Mixer](https://arxiv.org/abs/2105.01601) and [FlexiViT](https://arxiv.org/abs/2212.08013). Also trying to understand deep learning and make it simpler, [patient and consistent distillation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2106.05237), effective image-text pretraining with [locked image tower](https://arxiv.org/abs/2111.07991) or [sigmoid loss](https://arxiv.org/abs/2303.15343). And much more in [Google Scholar](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=H9I0CVwAAAAJ&hl=en).

I also enjoy writing flexible and performant research infrastructure. Open-sourcing the research infra I write is even more fun: [big_vision](https://github.com/google-research/big_vision).

Previously, I did my PhD at [ISTA](https://ist.ac.at/en/home/) under the supervision of [Christoph Lampert](https://cvml.ista.ac.at/), where I was working on weakly-supervised learning and generative image models.
